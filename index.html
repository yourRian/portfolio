My Artistic Journey

My artistic journey began in the quiet corners of elementary school, where pencil and paper were my closest companions. As a shy, introverted soul, I found solace in drawing delicate flowers that bloomed across my pages. High school opened new horizons as I discovered the art of drawing people and exploring different subjects.

By senior high school, I had fallen in love with portraiture,a passion that continues to flourish today in college. My specialty lies in creating detailed portraits that don't just show how someone looks, but who they truly are. Each pencil stroke, each shade, and each highlight is carefully placed to reveal the unique character and emotion of my subjects.

Now, as an I.T. student, I find balance between technology and art, turning to portrait drawing whenever I complete my academic work. When I'm not coding or studying, you'll find me bringing faces to life with each careful stroke, experimenting with new techniques, or sharing my passion for art with others. I believe that every face tells a story, and I'm honored to be the one who gets to tell it.
